About Us - Project Info and Legal matters

Pyrite OLAP Frontend is the frontend for Pyrite OLAP.

Pyrite OLAP, being developed by Krzysztof Sobolewski is a revival and expansion of the brilliant Cubes OLAP Framework by Stefan Urbanek.
